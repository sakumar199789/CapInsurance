What is Pet Insurance?

Pet insurance pays, partly or in total, for veterinary treatment of theinsured person's ill or injured pet. We will pay out when the pet dies, or if the pet is lost or stolen. Pet insurance
helps you cover expensive medical bills if your pet becomes ill or injured. You can also get reimbursed for preventative care, such as vaccinations and checkups, depending on the
coverages you choose. People frequently buy pet insurance plans for their dog or cat to help cover the costs of emergency medical care or serious illnesses, including cancer treatment.
